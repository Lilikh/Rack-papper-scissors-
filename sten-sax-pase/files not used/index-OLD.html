Rules

Welcome to Paw, Nose, Tail!
The game to find out who is the goodest boy of them all! The rules are simple.

  • Choose between Paw,Nose or Tail at the start of the round!
  • Paw SWATS away Tail!
  • Nose SNIFFS the Tail!
  • Tail SLAPS the Paw!
  • Upon draw, you go again until one wins the round!
  • First to five wins!
